How it works

Three simple steps

1

Place

Place the sheet of adhesive covering material on the processing table. It is centered using the alignment guide on the table.

2

Cover

Position the book against the vertical alignment guide, then slide it down onto the surface of the adhesive so the spine of the book makes firm contact.

3

Finish

The CoverPro covers the book quickly and quietly, bubble and wrinkle free. The book exits the chute, ready for finishing.
